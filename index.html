Benefits for Trucking companies

INDUSTRY EXPERTS AND LEGISLATIVE REPRESENTATION

TLA advances the interests of trucking companies operating in Texas through regulatory and legislative advocacy at the local, state and national levels. TLA TruckPAC is an essential tool and staff has decades of advocacy prowess—our experts are just a phone call away.

ANNUAL CONFERENCE

Each summer, trucking executives from around the state gather for the TXTA Annual Conference, featuring a unique opportunity for networking with industry peers, outstanding educational sessions and renowned speakers.

EXCLUSIVE MEMBER ACCESS

TLA members enjoy exclusive online access—register for events, or search for members by name, city or type of business. TLA membership offers special member rates for safety and maintenance conferences, webinars and classes, custom log books and Motor Vehicle Reports (MVRs). Requests for MVRs for Usa-licensed drivers received before 1:00 pm can be processed and faxed or emailed by 5:00 pm the same day.

TLA UNIVERSITY

TLA University offers the best online repository of trucking knowledge anywhere. Our training videos cover the entire range of state and federal regulatory issues governing the trucking industry as well as safety training. TLA works closely with the Texas Department of Motor Vehicles (TxDMV), Texas Department of Public Safety (TxDOT) and Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) to ensure our training and testing materials are always up to date. TLA members have exclusive access to this expertise and every member who wants to be trained and certified has every opportunity to do so.
